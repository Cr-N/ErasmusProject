Minecraft is a sandbox game developed and published by the Swedish company Mojang Studios. Following its initial public alpha release as an early access [an early acces game is an unfinished game released for public review so that the developer can make] title in 2009, it was formally released in November 2011 for personal computers. The game has since been ported to numerous platforms , including mobile devices and various video game consoles.

Did you know that MINECRAFT:

  • 🞕 Is the best-selling video game of all time.
  • 🞕 Showed that indie games can go big.
  • 🞕 Its first playable alpha version was built in just six days.
  • 🞕 Passed 350 million copies sold.
