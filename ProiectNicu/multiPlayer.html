Multiplayer lets you play Minecraft with other people. Instead of exploring the world alone, you can build, survive, fight, trade, and create with friends or players from around the world.
There are many ways to play multiplayer. Some players join survival servers where everyone builds bases and gathers resources together. Others play minigames like BedWars, SkyWars, Parkour, Survival Games, or Capture the Flag. Some servers focus on roleplay, economy, factions, or huge creative building projects.

Why Multiplayer is fun

Minecraft multiplayer is fun because every player can have a different role. One person can build the base, another can mine, another can farm, and another can explore. Working together makes big projects easier and more exciting.
