The teckel, also known as the dachshund, is a small dog breed originally developed in Germany for hunting badgers and other burrowing animals. Despite its short legs and long long body, the teckel is brave, energetic, and very intelligent.

It has a strong sense of smell and a curious personality, making it both a skilled hunting dog and a loyal companion.
Teckels can have short, long, or wire-haired coats, and they are known for being affectionate with their families while sometimes showing a stubborn side.
